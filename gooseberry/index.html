The Story Behind Gooseberry

There’s something about Gooseberry that instantly feels different — real, intimate, and full of soul. The brand was born in Bali back in 2012, when its founder, a European fashion creative, couldn’t find lingerie that felt both chic and easy to wear. So she decided to make her own. What came out of that mix of Parisian polish and island freedom became Gooseberry’s signature — a balance of elegance, simplicity, and a touch of quiet confidence.

What started as a small, handmade lingerie project has grown into a global favorite — yet it still feels personal. Gooseberry is now run by the founder’s daughters, who’ve kept the same heart behind the brand: helping women feel beautiful, empowered, and completely themselves.

Every piece feels like a love letter to craftsmanship. From delicate lace to perfectly cut silhouettes, each design is made in collaboration with Balinese artisans who put care into every stitch. It’s slow fashion in the most beautiful sense — intentional, thoughtful, and meant to last.

And while Gooseberry started with lingerie, it’s naturally evolved into something bigger. Their swim and ready-to-wear pieces carry the same effortless DNA — sun-soaked, confident, and always a little romantic. The Seaside Collection especially captures that Bali energy: golden beaches, soft fabrics, and a sense of ease that makes you want to pack a suitcase and chase the sun.

But what really makes Gooseberry special is its heart. The brand doesn’t just talk about sustainability — it lives it. Every piece is made with fair trade practices, and for each order, Gooseberry helps remove a pound of plastic waste from the ocean. It’s full-circle poetic: giving back to the same sea that inspires so much of what they create.

At its core, Gooseberry is more than fashion. It’s about how you feel — strong, soft, confident, and completely at home in your own skin.

Design & Materials

What makes Gooseberry truly unforgettable is the way each piece feels — not just how it looks. There’s a quiet confidence in their designs, a sense that every detail has a purpose. Nothing is overcomplicated, yet everything feels intentional.

Gooseberry’s signature aesthetic blends minimalism and sensuality in a way that feels both fresh and timeless. The cuts are clean, the lines flattering, and the fits designed to hug the body in all the right places. You’ll notice little touches that make a big difference — scalloped lace edges, sheer mesh panels, subtle underwiring for support, and dainty straps that feel barely there.

When it comes to materials, quality is everything. Each collection uses premium lace, silky satins, and breathable fabrics that feel soft against the skin. The craftsmanship reflects the brand’s attention to detail — from hand-finished seams to precision stitching that ensures comfort and durability. Even their swimwear, though designed to turn heads, feels surprisingly functional, offering both stretch and structure for the perfect fit.

The overall effect is effortless. Gooseberry pieces don’t just flatter your body — they move with it. You can feel the difference when you wear them: the lightness of the lace, the smoothness of the silk, the confidence that comes from knowing you’re wearing something made with care. It’s that combination of beauty and comfort that makes Gooseberry’s design philosophy so timeless — and so loved.
